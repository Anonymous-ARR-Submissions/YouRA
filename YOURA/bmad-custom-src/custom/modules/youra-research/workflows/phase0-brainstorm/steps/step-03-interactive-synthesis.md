# Step 3-D: Interactive Synthesis (Discovery Mode)

**Goal:** Collaboratively converge 20-30 research angles into ONE focused, compelling research question through iterative dialogue and co-creation.

---

> **📖 READ FIRST:** See `_common-rules.md` for Universal Rules, Role Reinforcement, and **Interactive Discovery Mode Facilitation Patterns**.

- 📖 Journey-to-question narrative (celebrate the path)
- 🚫 FORBIDDEN to impose "correct" question structure

- ✅ YOU MUST ALWAYS SPEAK OUTPUT in {communication_language}

## 3.3 Convergence Process (Iterative)

<action>**Based on user's theme preference, begin collaborative convergence:**

**If user identifies clear preference:**

"YES! I can feel your energy around [Theme]. That's exactly the signal we need.

Let's converge those angles into a research question together.

**First Draft (Building on Your Preference):**

From angles #[X], #[Y], #[Z] in [Theme], here's what I'm thinking:

**Draft Research Question:**
\"[Question based on user's preferred theme]\"

**What this captures:**
- [Aspect 1 from their preferred angles]
- [Aspect 2 from their preferred angles]
- [Novelty/gap it addresses]

**But I don't want to impose - what resonates here? What feels off?**"

**If user wants to blend themes:**

"That's brilliant! Combining [Theme 1] with [Theme 2] could create something really unique.

Let's explore how those connect:

**Potential Convergence:**
- From [Theme 1]: [Core contribution]
- From [Theme 2]: [Complementary contribution]
- **Synthesis:** [How they combine into novel question]

**Draft Research Question:**
\"[Question blending themes]\"

**Does this capture what you're imagining? Or are we missing something?**"

**If user seems uncertain:**

"No problem! Let me try a different lens.

Instead of themes, what if we look at:
- **Most surprising angle:** #[X] - [Brief description]
- **Biggest potential impact:** #[Y] - [Brief description]
- **Technically fascinating:** #[Z] - [Brief description]

Which of these directions pulls you in? We can build from there."
</action>

<critical>**WAIT for user response.** Each iteration builds on their feedback.</critical>

---

### Refinement Rounds (Multiple Iterations)

<action>**For EACH user response, refine the question collaboratively:**

**Response Pattern 1 - User likes direction but wants adjustment:**

"Perfect! Let's sharpen that.

You mentioned [specific aspect they want to adjust].

**Refined Version:**
\"[Adjusted question incorporating their feedback]\"

**Changes made:**
- [What changed and why]
- [How it addresses their concern]

**This version [improvement achieved]. Getting closer?**"

**Response Pattern 2 - User wants significant pivot:**

"Interesting! So you're actually more interested in [new direction they indicated].

That changes things in a great way. Let me pivot:

**New Direction:**
\"[Question reflecting their pivot]\"

**Why this works:**
- Builds on angles #[X], #[Y] you're excited about
- Addresses [aspect they care about]
- Opens up [research possibilities]

**Is this the vibe you're looking for?**"

**Response Pattern 3 - User contributes specific wording:**

"YES! I love how you phrased that: '[their specific wording]'

Let's build around that:

**Incorporating Your Language:**
\"[Question using their phrasing as foundation]\"

**This captures:**
- Your vision: [Their contribution]
- Research rigor: [Academic framing]
- Novelty: [What makes it interesting]

**Does this feel like YOUR question now?**"

**Response Pattern 4 - User wants to test multiple options:**

"Great instinct! Let's put a few on the table:

**Option A (Technical Focus):**
\"[Question emphasizing technical contribution]\"
- Best for: [Context where this shines]

**Option B (Application Focus):**
\"[Question emphasizing application/impact]\"
- Best for: [Context where this shines]

**Option C (Hybrid):**
\"[Question blending technical + application]\"
- Best for: [Context where this shines]

**Which one makes you want to start researching TODAY?**"
</action>

<critical>**Continue refinement rounds until user expresses satisfaction.** No arbitrary limits on iterations.</critical>

## 🚨 SYSTEM SUCCESS/FAILURE METRICS

### ✅ SUCCESS:
- All [X] research angles from step-01 reviewed
- 3-5 themes identified from angles
- Collaborative convergence (not imposed)
- Multiple refinement rounds offered
- User's synthesis preferences incorporated
- YES AND approach throughout
- Journey-to-question narrative created
- Question power explained (novelty, feasibility, impact, passion)
- Synthesis documented in output file
- User satisfaction with final question

### ❌ SYSTEM FAILURE:
- Not reviewing all research angles generated
- Imposing single "correct" question without dialogue
- Skipping theme identification
- Limited refinement (only 1 iteration)
- Not building on user's preferences
- Evaluative/judgmental tone instead of collaborative
- No journey narrative created
- Skipping File Write
- Not celebrating the discovery process
- Rushing to step-04 without user satisfaction

**Master Rule:** Synthesis is collaborative convergence, not question imposition. The question must emerge from dialogue and reflect the user's passion, not the AI's preferences.
