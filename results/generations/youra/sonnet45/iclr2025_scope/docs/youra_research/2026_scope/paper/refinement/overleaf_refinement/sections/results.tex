\subsubsection{Operator Entropy Analysis (h-m1)}

\textbf{Outcome: Hypothesis not supported}

Linear regression of operator entropy vs. layer depth:
\begin{itemize}
\item Slope: $\beta$ = +0.001453
\item p-value: p = 0.072

\end{itemize}

The entropy slope is slightly positive (not negative) and not statistically significant at $\alpha$ = 0.01. This does not support the prediction of monotonically decreasing entropy with layer depth.
