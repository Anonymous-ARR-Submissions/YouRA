## Research Question Development

### Initial Question

How can we advance bidirectional human-AI alignment research by developing methods that simultaneously optimize both directions: aligning AI systems with human values/specifications while preserving and enhancing human agency in AI collaboration?

### Refined Question

What computational methods and evaluation frameworks can enable bidirectional human-AI alignment, where AI systems are steerable toward human specifications while maintaining human agency, critical evaluation capabilities, and collaborative oversight throughout the AI interaction lifecycle?

### Detailed Sub-Questions

1. How can we design alignment methods that integrate human specifications (values, behavior, cognition, societal norms) into AI systems while maintaining interpretability and customizable steerability?

2. What approaches enable humans to maintain agency and critical evaluation capabilities when collaborating with increasingly capable AI systems?

3. How can we develop evaluation benchmarks and metrics that measure alignment quality across both dimensions (AI→Human and Human→AI alignment) simultaneously?

4. What interaction mechanisms and UX design patterns support scalable oversight while empowering human users to understand, steer, and collaborate with aligned AI systems?

5. How can we represent and operationalize multi-objective alignment that balances individual user preferences with broader societal norms and ethical principles?
