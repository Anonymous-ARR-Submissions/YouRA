\textbf{Gate result:} H-E1 MUST_WORK gate satisfied (mean ρ = 0.3662 < threshold 0.70). This result supports the core motivation for joint training.

\textbf{Methodological note:} The GQA expansion (\texttt{repeat_interleave(4)}, treating 8 KV heads as 32 independent query-head signals) may inflate the perceived misalignment by reducing the effective correlation measure. A KV-head-level analysis (at 8 heads without expansion) may yield higher ρ values and is identified as a robustness check for future work.
