\subsubsection{Adapter Generation}

A total of 40 adapters were trained (8 tasks $\times$ 5 seeds) on TinyLlama-1.1B-Chat-v1.0. Training was conducted on NVIDIA H100 NVL, taking approximately 2 hours 14 minutes total.

\subsubsection{Evaluation Protocol}

For each adapter pair, B matrices were extracted for all 154 layer-module combinations (22 layers $\times$ 7 module types). Grassmann distance was computed per layer and aggregated using mean pooling across layers.

\subsubsection{Success Criteria}

\begin{itemize}
\item RQ1 (Existence): p < 0.05 and Cohen's d > 0.5
\item RQ2 (Mechanism): Spearman $\rho$ > 0.3 with p < 0.05
\item RQ3 (Layer Analysis): At least one layer type with Cohen's d > 0.8
\item P3 (Control): Within-task variance ratio < 0.5
\end{itemize}
