
\subsubsection{4.1 Models}

We evaluate instruction-tuned models and their base counterparts from two families:

\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Qwen:} Qwen2.5-7B (base) vs. Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct
\item \textbf{Mistral:} Mistral-7B-v0.1 (base) vs. Mistral-7B-Instruct-v0.2
\end{itemize}

The Llama-2-7B family was planned but not tested due to HuggingFace model gating restrictions at experiment time.

\subsubsection{4.2 Dataset}

We use MMLU \citep{Hendrycks2021MMLU} with 14,042 test samples across 57 subjects. This multiple-choice benchmark enables clean extraction of answer-token logits and provides ground-truth correctness labels.

\subsubsection{4.3 Inference Protocol}

All models use greedy decoding (temperature=0) with identical zero-shot prompts. We extract the top-1 and top-2 logits at the answer position for margin computation.

\subsubsection{4.4 Statistical Methods}

\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{AUROC comparison:} Bootstrap confidence intervals (n=1,000)
\item \textbf{Conditional margin tests:} Permutation tests (n=9,999, independent samples)
\item \textbf{Slope comparison:} Paired bootstrap (n=1,000)
\item \textbf{Brier decomposition:} Bootstrap confidence intervals (n=1,000) with 15 probability bins
\end{itemize}
