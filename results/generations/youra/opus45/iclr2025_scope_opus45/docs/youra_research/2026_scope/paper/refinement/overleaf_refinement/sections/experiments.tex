\subsubsection{4.2 Dataset}

WikiText-103 served as the evaluation corpus for language modeling perplexity. Perplexity was evaluated at context lengths of 25, 64, 128, 256, 512, and 1024 tokens.

\textbf{Limitation:} WikiText-103 does not provide controllable dependency lengths. A synthetic task such as Multi-Query Associative Recall (MQAR) would enable more precise testing of the H\_spec boundary but was not used in these experiments.
